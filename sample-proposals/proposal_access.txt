PROPOSAL #006 — Grant Emergency Pause Authority to 3-of-5 Security Multisig

Summary
-------
Introduce a PAUSER_ROLE into the protocol's AccessControl registry and assign it
to a newly created 3-of-5 Gnosis Safe multisig (0xPauserSafe). Role holders may
call pause() on the four highest-value protocol contracts — LiquidityPool,
StakingVault, GovernanceV1, and BridgeAdapter — but may not modify parameters,
upgrade logic, or access treasury funds.

Multisig Composition
--------------------
The five keyholders are drawn from distinct operational teams to prevent
collusion: two core engineers, one security researcher, one external auditor
partner, and one elected community representative (term: 6 months, renewable).
No single entity may hold more than one key.

Pause Mechanics
---------------
- Trigger   : Any 3-of-5 signers may initiate a pause with a signed on-chain
  transaction; no off-chain coordination is sufficient.
- Scope     : pause() suspends deposit, withdrawal, and vote-casting functions.
  Read-only views and existing queued timelock operations are unaffected.
- Auto-expiry: A paused state automatically lifts after 72 hours unless a full
  DAO governance vote (quorum: 10%, simple majority) explicitly extends it.
- Unpause   : Any 3-of-5 signers may unpause before the 72-hour expiry.
  The DAO may also force-unpause via governance vote at any time.

Accountability
--------------
Within 24 hours of any pause event, the multisig must publish a public
incident report detailing the threat, evidence, and remediation timeline.
Failure to publish forfeits the PAUSER_ROLE pending a DAO re-vote.
All pause and unpause transactions are emitted as indexed on-chain events and
visible in the protocol's public dashboard.

Limitations
-----------
PAUSER_ROLE explicitly does NOT grant: upgrade authority, fund movement,
parameter changes, or the ability to pause the DAO's own voting contract.
Granting any of these additional capabilities requires a separate proposal.

Voting Period  : 72 hours
Quorum Required: 20% of total token supply
Passing Threshold: Supermajority (>66% weighted Yes)
