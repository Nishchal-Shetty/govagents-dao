PROPOSAL #004 — Liquidity Mining Program: 15% Treasury Allocation over 6 Months

Summary
-------
Allocate 15% of the DAO treasury (approximately 63,000 USDC at current balance of
420,000 USDC) to a six-month liquidity mining program on Uniswap v3, targeting
the GOV/USDC pair at the 0.3% fee tier. Rewards are distributed weekly via a
MasterChef-style staking contract deployed at a new audited address.

Program Parameters
------------------
- Total reward budget : 63,000 USDC disbursed in 26 equal weekly tranches
- Target pool        : Uniswap v3 GOV/USDC, 0.3% fee tier
- Price range        : ±20% around the 7-day TWAP at program start
- Minimum lock-up    : 7 days per LP position to qualify for rewards
- Reward token       : USDC (no new GOV token minting)
- Staking contract   : to be deployed and audited before the start block

Risk Controls
-------------
- A 3-of-5 treasury multisig must sign each weekly disbursement transaction.
- An independent on-chain oracle (Chainlink GOV/USD) guards against price
  manipulation; rewards are paused if spot price deviates >30% from TWAP.
- Unspent USDC after week 26 is automatically returned to the treasury.
- A governance vote is required to extend or modify the program beyond month 6.

Accountability
--------------
The proposer will publish weekly TVL, volume, and fee-revenue reports to the DAO
forum. If cumulative protocol fee revenue attributable to the program does not
reach 20,000 USDC by month 3, the DAO may vote to terminate early.

Voting Period  : 96 hours
Quorum Required: 15% of total token supply
Passing Threshold: Simple majority (>50% weighted Yes)
