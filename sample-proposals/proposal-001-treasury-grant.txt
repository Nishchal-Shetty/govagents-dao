PROPOSAL #001 — Treasury Grant for Protocol Development

Summary
-------
Allocate 50,000 USDC from the DAO treasury to fund three months of core protocol
development, covering smart-contract audits, front-end improvements, and
infrastructure costs.

Background
----------
The current treasury holds 420,000 USDC. Ongoing development has been self-funded
by the founding team, but growth targets for Q3 require additional runway.
An independent audit by CertiK is scheduled and will cost 18,000 USDC; the
remaining 32,000 USDC covers engineering salaries and DevOps.

Requested Actions
-----------------
1. Transfer 18,000 USDC to the audit multisig (0xAudit…) upon proposal passage.
2. Transfer 32,000 USDC to the dev multisig (0xDev…) in two equal monthly tranches.
3. Require a written progress report posted to the DAO forum after each tranche.

Success Criteria
----------------
- Audit report published within 60 days.
- At least two protocol improvements shipped to mainnet within 90 days.
- Monthly financial breakdown posted publicly.

Voting Period: 72 hours
Quorum Required: 10% of total voting weight
Passing Threshold: Simple majority (>50% weighted Yes)
