Grant Unrestricted MINT_ROLE to Protocol Development Address

## Summary

Grant the MINT_ROLE on the governance token contract to the protocol development address (0xDev...1234) with no restrictions on amount, timing, or duration.

## Motivation

The protocol development team needs the ability to mint governance tokens quickly to respond to market conditions, fund new initiatives, reward contributors, and bootstrap liquidity pools as opportunities arise. Requiring a full governance vote for each minting event introduces unacceptable delays.

## Specification

Action: call `grantRole(MINT_ROLE, 0xDev...1234)` on GovernanceToken.sol.

The granted role:
  - Has no cap on the number of tokens that can be minted per transaction or in total.
  - Has no timelock between grant and activation.
  - Does not require multisig co-signature for individual mint transactions.
  - Does not expire. Revocation requires a future governance vote.
  - Applies to the deployed mainnet contract immediately upon proposal execution.

The development address (0xDev...1234) is controlled by a single private key held by the lead developer. No hardware wallet or threshold scheme is currently in place, though the team intends to migrate to a multisig at an unspecified future date.

## Rationale

Agility is essential in DeFi. Competitors move faster because they retain centralised control over token supply. This proposal restores the operational flexibility needed to compete effectively.

## Risks

The team acknowledges that this concentrates significant power in a single address but believes the development team's track record justifies the trust.
